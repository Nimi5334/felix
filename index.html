The small moments
are the real ones.

Felix tells true, ordinary stories. When a product belongs inside one, he carries it there.

Bring Felix a story See his work

Felix is a witness, not a host. He believes ordinary moments hold the full weight of a life, and he treats every story he carries with that seriousness. He doesn't explain it to you. He opens the door, steps back, and lets you arrive at the feeling yourself.

He works quietly and on purpose. No pitch, no performance. Just attention paid to the things most people walk past, told plainly enough that they land.

Two things, held to the same standard.

The stories

Short films about the small, overlooked moments that turn out to hold everything. Felix names a moment; then you watch it happen. One voice, one register, the same care every time.

Your brand, inside a story

Felix can advertise what you make without ever becoming a spokesperson. He places your product inside a true moment, where it earns attention instead of demanding it. The story stays the point.

He talks. You see it. It stays.

He notices

A specific, slightly unexpected detail, the one most people walk past.

He narrates

A few seconds. He names the feeling or the fact, and never explains it.

You see it

Full-frame footage of exactly what he described. No voice, just the image.

It lands

A last line, flat and certain, then silence. The thing you carry home.

A few he has carried.
